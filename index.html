About Shanghai Summer School

Like American entrepreneur, author and motivational speaker Jim Rohn once said, “You are the average of the five people you most associate with.”

So join us at Shanghai Summer School this summer, and surround yourself with new friends who will motivate you to keep growing and succeeding.

Engaging Chinese Language classes, informative business lectures and field trips, and a myriad of cultural activities. All of these take place in the throbbing metropolis that is Shanghai.

How we see it

Life is one big journey. Friends we make and memories that we create make up a large part of that journey, and are what makes the journey interesting and worthwhile.

We see Shanghai Summer school as more than just a month-long summer activity, but as an experience that connects people from all over the world on a different level to create memories that will last a lifetime. Come and be a part of our Global Family.
